ICON INSTRUCTIONS
=================

Replace icon.png with your own logo (256x256 PNG).

The build process needs:
- icon.png  (256x256, used for Linux/Mac and as source)
- icon.ico  (Windows — auto-generated from icon.png if you use electron-builder)

To convert PNG to ICO automatically, electron-builder handles this.
Or use: https://convertio.co/png-ico/

Your logo should be:
- Square (1:1 ratio)
- At least 256x256 pixels
- PNG format with transparent background preferred
